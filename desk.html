DESK

Downtown Evening Soup Kitchen

Since 1987, DESK (Downtown Evening Soup Kitchen) has served New Haven's unhoused and food-insecure residents with hot meals seven days a week, alongside a drop-in resource center, food pantry, and community outreach programs. Last year alone, DESK served over 4,300 people and distributed more than 113,000 meals and groceries, while connecting individuals to pathways toward stability and housing. They are one of New Haven's most vital and enduring pillars of compassionate community care.
