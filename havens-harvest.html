Haven's Harvest

Rescuing Food, Nourishing Communities

Haven's Harvest is a food rescue organization that recovers surplus food from donors and delivers it free of charge to underserved communities across Greater New Haven, the Lower Naugatuck Valley, and New London regions. With a volunteer network of 470 people, Haven's Harvest has rescued over 10 million pounds of food since 2019 — valued at $55.3 million — distributing it through 660 sites including schools, senior centers, and nonprofits. Their work simultaneously fights hunger and reduces food waste, building a more equitable and sustainable food system for all.
