Root-Life

Living the Change We Wish to See

Root Life is a Black-led urban farming and environmental education organization in New Haven, dedicated to "Living The Change We Wish To See." Through workshops, healing retreats, and community education, they teach both youth and adults how to grow food and medicine while reconnecting people to nature and indigenous wellness practices. With over 10 years of experience and permaculture design certification, Root Life weaves together practical agricultural training and holistic healing rooted in cultural relevance and sustainability.
