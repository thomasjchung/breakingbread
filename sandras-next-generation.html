Sandra's Next Generation

A Neighborhood Table

Sandra's Next Generation is a beloved New Haven restaurant offering soulful, home-cooked meals, catering, private events, and cooking classes rooted in community and culture. A cornerstone of the Dixwell neighborhood, the restaurant has long been a gathering place where food brings people together across generations. Sandra's is more than a restaurant — it's a testament to the power of nourishing your community one meal at a time.
